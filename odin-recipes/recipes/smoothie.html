PB&J Smoothie

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make a peanut butter and jelly smoothie in under 3 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 medium banana
  • 1⁄2 cup milk
  • 2 Tbsp plain yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Add all indgredients to a blender and blend for 10-20 seconds
